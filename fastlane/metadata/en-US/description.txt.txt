Step into Wispwood, an atmospheric glow-runner where light is your only lifeline. Guide your spirit wisp through a mysterious shadow forest, evade ancient dark beasts, and gather glowing light-orbs to illuminate your path.

Master the rhythm of the woods with smooth, responsive controls. Tap to leap over ground obstacles, or swipe down to slide beneath floating spirits. Chain light-orbs together to trigger a powerful combo aura and boost your high score.

Choose your favorite spirit wisp, each with unique survival perks:
• Wisp: The classic, perfectly balanced traveler.
• Ember: Sturdy and resilient, starting your journey with extra hearts.
• Lumen: Radiant aura that keeps your high-scoring combo active longer.
• Zephyr: Nimble and swift, pulling nearby orbs with a wider pickup reach.

How far can you run before the shadows claim the light?

Key Features:
• Beautiful, neon-atmospheric visuals with immersive ambient soundscapes.
• Simple yet addictive tap-and-slide mechanics.
• Multiple unlockable characters with distinct gameplay advantages.
• Compete with your personal best and chase the ultimate high score.
